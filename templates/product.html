Box Bag

The box shape provides added stability, allowing the bag to stand upright on its own, making it convenient for packing groceries or other items. Non-woven box bags are eco-friendly alternatives to single-use plastic bags, offering a reusable and sustainable option for shopping and carrying goods.

Tshirt Bags
Tshirt Bag

Non-woven t-shirt bags are lightweight, durable, and eco-friendly alternatives to traditional plastic bags. Made from spunbond non-woven polypropylene fabric, these bags are strong enough to carry heavy items while being reusable and recyclable.

Die-cut Bag
Die-cut Bag

The DieCut design features handles that are cut directly into the bag material, providing a seamless and sturdy carrying solution. These bags are versatile and can be customized with various colors, sizes, and designs to suit specific needs, making them ideal for shopping, promotions, events, and more.

Handle Bag
Handle Bag

Non-woven handle bags are eco-friendly alternatives to traditional plastic bags. Made from spunbond polypropylene fabric, these bags are durable, reusable, and recyclable. They offer a sturdy handle for easy carrying and come in various sizes and colors.
