Understanding Non-Woven Bags

Non-woven bags are made from spun-bonded polypropylene (PP) fabric, which is lightweight, durable, and recyclable. Unlike traditional plastic bags, which are made from petroleum-based materials and take centuries to decompose, non-woven bags are biodegradable and can break down within a few years under the right conditions. This makes them a much more sustainable option for everyday use.

Environmental Impact

The environmental impact of plastic bags is well-documented, with millions ending up in landfills and oceans each year, posing a severe threat to marine life and ecosystems. Non-woven bags offer a solution to this problem by reducing reliance on single-use plastics and minimizing pollution. By choosing non-woven bags, individuals and businesses can actively contribute to reducing plastic waste and preserving the planet for future generations.

Versatility and Customization

One of the key advantages of non-woven bags is their versatility. They come in various sizes, shapes, and colors, making them suitable for a wide range of applications. From grocery shopping and promotional events to corporate giveaways and retail packaging, non-woven bags can be customized to meet specific needs and branding requirements.
